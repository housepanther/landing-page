Welcome to Big Cat World
Big Cat World is a feline research, conservation, and captivity breeding NGO dedicated to preservation of our majestic wild cats.

Meet the Big Cats

This lions is looking regal. Thankfully the lion population is remaining relatively stable in Africa.

Tigers live in a variety of different ecosystems from the jungles to the mountains. Tiger populations in India and Bhutan are recovering!

Leopards can be found primarily in jungles and tropical climates. Unfortunately, they are on the endangered spieces list due to human encroachment and habitat reduction.
